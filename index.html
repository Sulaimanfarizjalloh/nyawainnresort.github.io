Discover Our Services

Travel Plan

Prepare to embark on a journey like no other with Nyawa Inn Resort Hotel. We understand that every traveler is unique, and our goal is to create tailor-made travel plans that will make your stay with us an unforgettable adventure.

Catering Service

When it comes to hosting a memorable event, the right catering service can make all the difference. Nyawa Inn Resort Hotel is delighted to offer a world-class catering service that brings exceptional cuisine and impeccable service to your special occasions.

Babysitting

At Nyawa Inn Resort Hotel, we understand that every moment of your stay should be worry-free, including when you need some adult time. That's why we offer a reliable and professional babysitting service to ensure your children are safe and well-cared for while you enjoy the resort's amenities.

Laundry

At Nyawa Inn Resort Hotel, we understand that travel should be a time of relaxation and ease. That's why we offer a convenient and reliable laundry service to ensure that you can focus on enjoying your stay while we take care of your laundry needs.

Hire Driver

At Nyawa Inn Resort Hotel, we believe in providing a seamless and stress-free experience for our guests. To enhance your stay, we offer a dependable driver service, ensuring you can explore the area with ease and safety.

Bar & Drink

Elevate your stay at Nyawa Inn Resort Hotel by indulging in our exceptional bar and drink offerings. Whether you're seeking a refreshing beverage after a day of adventure or a place to unwind and celebrate, our bars provide the perfect setting for memorable moments.
